The St. Leticia Project

In Ndalama Village of Central Province Zambia the New Secondary School St. Leticia brings Community Development & Empowerment through Education.

Background:
Ndalama Village Secondary School, situated in Central Province, Zambia, has been a beacon of education in the region. However, to keep up with the demands of the modern world, the school recognized the need for an upgraded and well-maintained computer infrastructure. In response to this, Afriactron Technologies, and Infy Tech Sys Solution joined hands to contribute their expertise in computer hardware and software maintenance.

Scope of Work:


The maintenance project covered a range of tasks aimed at improving the overall performance and longevity of the computer systems. The scope included:

  • Staff Computer Maintenance:

Dedicated attention to the eight staff computers, catering to specific needs and preferences.

Providing training on basic troubleshooting and system maintenance for ICT staff members.

  • Software Optimization:

    Updating operating systems to the latest versions.

      Installing essential software updates and patches.

      Ensuring the presence of reliable antivirus software for enhanced security.

      Impact on Education:


      The collaborative efforts of Afriactron Technologies, and Infy Tech Sys Solution have elevated the technological infrastructure at the Ndalama Village Secondary School. The outcomes include:

      • Efficient Teaching Practices:


      Educators can now incorporate technology seamlessly into their teaching methods, providing interactive and engaging lessons.
      • Enhanced Staff Productivity:


      The maintenance of staff computers ensures that administrative tasks are carried out efficiently, contributing to the overall productivity of the school.


      • Improved Learning Experience:


      Students now have access to reliable and updated computer systems, enhancing their learning experience in various subjects.



      Community Empowerment:


      The project serves as a testament to the positive impact that collaboration between technology firms and educational institutions can have on local communities, empowering them for a brighter future.
